About Us

The IIUC Computer Club is a student organization at the International Islamic University Chittagong (IIUC), dedicated to fostering a community of tech enthusiasts. The club focuses on enhancing members' knowledge and skills in the fields of computer science, programming, software development, and emerging technologies.
